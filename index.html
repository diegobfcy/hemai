How it works

Clip. Capture.
Diagnose.

Three steps from blood sample to clinical result. No lab. No specialist. No waiting days.

01

Attach. Capture.

A health worker clips the low-cost optical device onto their smartphone and photographs the blood smear. No specialized training needed — the process takes under a minute.

Works with any Android or iOS smartphone
02

AI analyzes the sample

The image is sent to our platform where computer vision models scan for disease markers — malaria parasites, anemia patterns, filariasis — processing in real time.

Works offline; syncs when connectivity is available
03

Results in under 3 minutes

The health worker receives a structured diagnostic result with confidence scores directly on their phone. Data is stored in our rural health database for epidemiological tracking.

96%+ accuracy validated in Peru pilot
